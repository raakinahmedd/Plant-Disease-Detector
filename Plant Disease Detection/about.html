About Project

The increasing global demand for food production poses significant challenges to farmers in ensuring crop yield and quality. Plant diseases are among the major factors affecting agricultural productivity, causing substantial economic losses and impacting food security. Early detection and timely intervention are crucial for effective disease management. Traditional methods of plant disease diagnosis and monitoring are often labor-intensive, time-consuming, and prone to human error. In recent years, advancements in artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision technologies have shown great potential in automating the Plant Disease Detection process.

This project aims to develop an innovative Plant Disease Detection system that utilizes AI algorithms and computer vision techniques to accurately identify and classify diseases in various crop species. The proposed system consists of three main stages: Real-time image acquisition, plant disease detection and disease classification. Firstly, high-resolution images of plant leaves showing symptoms of diseases are captured using digital cameras or smartphones. These images are then processed to extract relevant features and patterns using computer vision algorithms.
